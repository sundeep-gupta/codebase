Hi Geeks!!!
How many times were you in need of your colleague or friend next cubical to solve the bug in your code ? Well!!! I always need them atleast once a day. And many need me!!!

8 out of 10 questions answered by me required my very basic knowledge. Same might apply to the answer I recieve from others. Being an software geek!! I thought of applying the pareto principle to this problem.

I grouped 2 of my friends and when we analyzed the time spent solving the queries and getting answers to queries, the result was interesting. we saw that about 20% of our work-time is used by these trivia questions. This means more than an hour!!

We formed this website as an answer to save the time. Remember!!! Time once gone won't come back. This website is an intiative by the Bubble team towards sharing the day-to-day experiences of geeks like you and me.

Those who are looking for answer can post us the question through forums and get the query answered

You have information which you want to share, send it to us and it will be posted in articles section with your name

News section is all about the latest market knowledge in Computer industry.
