1. To set Networking properties on your System  run policytool (JAVA_HOME\bin\policytool.exe).
	i. Press Button "Add policy Entry".
	ii. In "policy Entry" window press Button "Add Permissions".
	iii. In "permissions" window change In first Combo box change "permission" to "all permissions".
	iv. click "ok" in "permissions" window.
	v. and click "done"in "policy Entry" window.
	vi. Select the file menu and choose "save as" and save  ".java.policy" in C:\Documents and Settings\administrator.
